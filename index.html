Our tea is one of the most popular drinks in the world

Tea, cherished for its diverse flavors and soothing qualities, ranks among the most popular beverages globally. It is enjoyed in various forms, from traditional green and black teas to innovative herbal blends, catering to a wide range of tastes and cultures. Its rich history and cultural significance have cemented its place as a daily ritual for millions of people around the world.

Daily use of a cup of tea is good for your health

A daily cup of tea can significantly benefit your health, thanks to its rich antioxidants and anti-inflammatory properties. Regular consumption has been linked to improved heart health, enhanced mental alertness, and a reduced risk of chronic diseases. Additionally, tea can support digestive health and provide a calming effect, contributing to overall well-being.

The history of tea leaf in the world

The history of the tea leaf begins in ancient China, where legend says Emperor Shen Nong discovered it in 2737 BCE when tea leaves accidentally fell into his boiling water. Tea cultivation and consumption spread to Japan by the 8th century and to India in the 19th century,

where the British established large plantations. By the 16th century, European traders introduced tea to the West, leading to its global proliferation and establishing it as one of the most popular beverages worldwide.
